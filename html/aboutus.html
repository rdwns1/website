About Pamana Plate

TIMELESS RECIPES, RICH TRADITIONS.

Who We Are

Pamana Plate is a digital hub dedicated to preserving and showcasing traditional Filipino cuisine. It highlights the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines, featuring recipes passed down through generations. The website explores iconic dishes like adobo, sinigang, and ginataan, alongside regional specialties such as bicol express, empanada, and kinilaw.

Each recipe is complemented by detailed cooking techniques, ingredient explanations, and the cultural stories that shape these dishes. Pamana Plate emphasizes the importance of Filipino food in fostering community, celebrating family traditions, and connecting with ancestral roots.

Additionally, the platform serves as a resource for understanding indigenous ingredients, sustainable cooking practices, and regional variations in Filipino cooking, providing both a cultural and practical guide to traditional Filipino gastronomy.

Our History

Pamana Plate began as a simple passion project by a group of friends dedicated to preserving the rich culinary traditions of the Philippines. Inspired by handwritten recipes passed down through generations, the site was created to document and share classic Filipino dishes.

Initially started as a small online recipe collection, Pamana Plate grew into a space where visitors could learn about the history and cultural significance of Filipino food. With a focus on authenticity and tradition, it remains a humble platform aimed at keeping the spirit of Filipino cooking alive for future generations.
