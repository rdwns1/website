Sarciadong Pechay

Like every sarciado dish, this has a great sauce made of tomatoes and eggs. Mix that with some refreshing pechay for a truly tasty dish. component.

12 MINS

Cooking Time

Easy

Difficulty

5 MINS

Prep Time

4 People

Serves

What is Sarciadog Pechay?

Sarciado dish, this has a great sauce made of tomatoes and eggs. We mix that with some refreshing pechay for a truly tasty dish. Sarciadong Pechay described it as one of their favorites because it is able to highlight the flavors of each of its components perfectly.

Ingredients

  • 3 bunches pechay sliced
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 5 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 onion minced
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste

Procedure

1

Heat oil in a pan. Sauté garlic, onion, and tomatoes.

2

Add the pechay. Sauté for 20 seconds and then pour water. Let it boil.

3

Gently pour the beaten egg around the pan. Stir and continue cooking until the eggs start to solidify.

4

Season with fish sauce and ground black pepper. Serve with rice.

5

Share and enjoy!
