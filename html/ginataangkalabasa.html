Ginataang Kalabasa

This Filipino dish is a made with kalabasa (squash) and malunggay (moringa) leaves simmered in gata (coconut cream).

12 MINS

Cooking Time

Easy

Difficulty

5 MINS

Prep Time

4 People

Serves

What is Ginataang Kalabasa with malunggay?

This ginataang kalabasa with malunggay recipe is a hearty dish that brings out the natural sweetness of kalabasa, and is made even more mouuthwatering with the savory creamy gata-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic finely finely chopped
  • 1 large red onion finely chopped
  • 3 cups kalabasa cubed, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 piece green chili (siling pangsigang) optional
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1 Cup fresh malunggay leaves
  • fish sauce patis
  • ground black pepper

Procedure

1

Heat oil in a cooking pot. Saute the garlic and onion until the onion becomes soft.

2

Add kalabasa and saut√© for 2 to 3 minutes.

3

Pour in the coconut milk. Stir and bring to a boil.

4

Cook for 10 minutes, and add the green chili and the coconut cream.

5

Cover and bring to a simmer slowly until the kalabasa is tender. (Do not boil the coconut cream.)!

6

Add malunggay leaves. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Add fish sauce and black pepper to taste.

7

Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve hot.
