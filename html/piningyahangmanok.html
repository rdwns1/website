Pininyahang Manok

Pininyahang Manok literally translates to pineapple chicken. This Recipe calls for chicken slices to be marinated in pineapple juice then stewed with pineapple chunks.

45 MINS

Cooking Time

Easy

Difficulty

10 MINS

Prep Time

6 People

Serves

What is Pininyahang Manok?

Pininyahang Manok, or pineapple chicken, is a well-known dish made of chicken that is cooked with a creamy sauce, usually using milk along with pineapple juice and its chunks.

Traditional recipes use coconut milk because of its thick consistency, but you can also use table cream or all-purpose cream as a substitute, which is what is used in this recipe.

Other ingredients include carrots, potatoes, bell peppers, and garlic and onions, among others.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 12 ounces pineapple chunks canned
  • 2 pieces tomato chopped
  • 1 cup bell pepper cut into thick strips
  • 1 piece carrot widged
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce patis
  • 1/2 cup fresh milk
  • 2 tablespoon garlic minced
  • 1 piece onion sliced
  • 2 tablespoon cooking oil

Procedure

1

Marinate the chicken in pineapple juice/concentrate (derived from the can of pineapple chunks) for 20 to 30 minutes

2

Pour the cooking oil in a cooking pot / casserole then apply heat

3

Saut√© the garlic, onion, and tomatoes

4

Put-in the chicken and cook until color of the outer part turns light brown

5

Add the pineapple juice/concentrate marinade and fresh milk then bring to a boil

5

Put-in the carrots and simmer for 5 minutes

5

Add the bell pepper and fish sauce then simmer for 3 minutes

5

Remove from the cooking pot / casserole and transfer to a serving dish.
