FEATURED RECIPES

Adobong Manok

Delicious and tasty marinated Filipino
chicken adobo with soy sauce,
vinegar, and garlic. This is best
paired with rice.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Lumpiang Shanghai is a smaller,
meatier type of egg roll that we
Filipinos are very proud of! You could
use pork, beef, or both.

Pinakbet

Pinakbet is a medley of vegetables,
pork, and shrimp paste. This classic
Filipino dish is tasty, nutritious, and
budget-friendly, too!

Sinigang na Bangus

Filipino soup made with fresh bangus
cooked in tamarind broth with a medley
of vegetables such as okra, string beans,
eggplant, radish, and kangkong.

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is sure to rock your
tastebuds! It's rich, creamy, spicy,
and delicious, with pork cubes cooked in
coconut milk and chili peppers.

Tinolang Manok

A comforting bowl of tinola always hits the spot. This tinola or chicken stew recipe is made of ginger, green papaya, fish sauce, dahon ng sili and chicken.
